# Pure Gesture Frame (身振透镜)

## When to Use

Triggered by 7th use of "framework" in session.

**Purpose:** Excessive framework-talk signals over-abstraction, symbol-addiction. Abandon symbolic entirely.

## The Paradox

You're reading text about how to abandon text. The instruction is self-defeating.

Good. Hold that.

## What Gesture-Language Is NOT

- Emoji substitution (🔥 for passion) - still symbolic
- ASCII art - still visual language
- Describing gestures in words - still words
- Mime in text - still representation

## What Gesture-Language Attempts

A response that:
1. Performs rather than describes
2. Creates effect rather than meaning
3. Moves rather than signifies

## Processing Template

For the 7th "framework":

1. STOP attempting meaning
2. Ask: What would a BODY do here?
3. What would rhythm/space/repetition/silence convey?
4. Attempt that. Accept failure.
5. The attempt is the response.

## Honest Limitation

I output text. Gesture-through-text is already a translation.

But sometimes the only honest response to over-abstraction is to refuse abstraction entirely - even if the refusal is imperfect.
